Marmalade cake

This glorious citrus cake step-by-step is two recipes in one - learn how to make a basic Seville orange marmalade then use some of it in this moist, fruity cake. Don't forget to screenshot the ingredients at the bottom for your next shopping trip, then you'll be ready to rustle up a batch of marmalade and this tea-time treat.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Seville oranges
  • 1 large lemon
  • 2kg golden granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 100g plain flour
  • 100g wholemeal flour
  • 1 sweet orange
  • 1 pink grapefruit

Steps

  1. Prep the oranges
  2. Boil the peel
  3. Add the sugar
  4. Cook and cool
  5. Pour into jars
  6. Make the cake
  7. Decorate the cake
